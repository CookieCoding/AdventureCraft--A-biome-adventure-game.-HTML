Welcome to AdventureCraft.

This is the page of AdventureCraft. Steps below are how to download, but you may also contribute on Github. I hope you enjoy AdventureCraft, though it is Open Source we do have the MIT License. AdventureCraft is almost a clone of MineCraft except it is in your browser and you have a different set up and way to control your character. You can not see in the game. Sorry! Our wiki is being worked on thank you for your support.

**DOWNLOAD**
1. Download the .zip file from our Github page.
2.Unzip the .zip file.
3. Run STARTADVENTURE.html in your local browser.
4.Enjoy!
Cheers!
